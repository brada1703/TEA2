{{ 'GRAMMAR.Interrogative' | translate }}

Interrogative adverbs are used to form a question. These are: why, when, where, and how.

Note that what, who, whom, which, and whose are interrogative pronouns.

Examples:

  • Why is the sky blue?
  • Why do I have ten fingers?
  • Where is Alaska?
  • Where can I find my dog?
  • When is mom coming home?
  • When is dinner ready?
  • How do you do that?
  • How can I do my homework if I have no pencil?
