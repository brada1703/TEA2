{{ 'GRAMMAR.Placement' | translate }}

Unlike most romance languages, adjectives are usually placed before the noun they modify in English.

Examples:

  • He has a green shirt.
  • We went to some lovely beaches.
  • She likes to drink Italian Spritz.
  • They ran in the Boston Marathon.
